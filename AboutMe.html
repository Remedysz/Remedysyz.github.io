About Me : Trevor Bradford

Trevor Bradford - Resume (PDF)

I am a student graduating in May of 2020 with a bachelor degree in computer science. I currently work at Amazon as a fulltime outbound problem solver associate where I troubleshoot and reduce "problem" shipments throughout the facility. Amazon provided me with the opportunity and accommodation to go to school for my degree and I hope to apply the knowledge I have learned by seeking full-time or internship positions within my field of study.

My main interests include development, whether it be software applications or web application. I love creating the interaction someone has with an application and the innovation behind it using new or old technologies to create something that provides a service or use. I hope in the future, I may lead a development team on a start up project for a company or funded personal project of my own where my creativity can really shape the end result.

Some of my hobbies include photography, kayaking, miniature painting, cartography, 3D sculpting, and 3D printing.
